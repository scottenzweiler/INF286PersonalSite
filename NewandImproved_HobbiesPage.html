Hobbies

Scott really enjoys almost all forms of physical activity. He thouroughly enjoys sports typically works out in a gym 5-6 times a week. He takes his physical and mental health very seriously, therefore working out is less of a hobby and more of a lifestyle to him. He has also competed in sanctioned USAPL powerlifting competitions where competitors compete in the squat, bench and deadlift exercises. At one time scott held a state record in the state of Ohio for his squat which was 324.3lbs. in the 145lb weight division.


Aside from the gym Scott also spends his free time enjoying many outdoor activities such as sport shooting, hunting, fishing, hiking, and kayaking.
